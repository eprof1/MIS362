Learning Objectives

These exercises introduce several concepts of operations management.

After completing this assignment you will be able to:

  1. Identify the major components of an operations management system
  2. Identify and distinguish development and operations work
  3. Identify and explain systems monitoring and event management
  4. Create a plan that factors operational feedback into product design
  5. Identify the important features and impact of dependency analysis on your organization
  6. Explain the importance of layered complexity in modern systems design
