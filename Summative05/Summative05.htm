Exercise 3-Open Source Kanban

There are several open source options for implementing kanban methodology in a distributed environment.

The power and dynamics of face to face interaction should not be discounted.
But a digital kanban board can be used in both face to face encounters, but also serves as a way make your board 'portable'.
There are positives and negatives associated with using a digital kanban board, the best way to find what works for you is through experience.

Read the reviews on five open source digital kanban tools by Jason Baker.
Discuss these tools with your group and class members.
