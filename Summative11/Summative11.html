Enterprise Information Management



600 Points
Learning Objectives

These exercises introduce the several concepts of Enterprise Information Management.

After completing this assignment you will be able to:

  1. Identify the major components of an Enterprise Information Management (EIM) system
  2. Identify the sources of value in an EIM
  3. Develop a framework for understanding EIM
  4. Identify the major components required to manage information resources
  5. Evaluate the relationship between EIM and Agile Information Management techniques
