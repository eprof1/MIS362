Organization and Culture



600 Points
Learning Objectives

These exercises introduce the concept of developing an organization's culture.

After completing this assignment you will be able to:

  1. Identify the major components that define an organization and its culture
  2. Identify how employees are grouped
  3. Identify the impact of the reporting structures
  4. Identify and distinguish functional versus product organizations
Assignment Overview

The goal of this assignment is to familiarize you with the factors that influence an organization and its culture.
