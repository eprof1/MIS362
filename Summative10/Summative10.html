Governance, Risk, Security and Compliance



850 Points
Learning Objectives

These exercises introduce governance, risk, security and compliance.

After completing this assignment you will be able to:

  1. Compare and contrast governance versus management
  2. Identify governance key objectives
  3. Identify risk management components
  4. Compare and contrast assurance and audit
  5. Identify digital security issues
  6. Identify regulatory compliance issues
  7. Evaluate the issues and effects of automating the digital delivery pipeline on governance

Exercise 4-Security

Security is an incredibly important issue for businesses that relay on digital delivery of services.
But clients and customers become irritated when security impedes convenience.
Do you enjoy being forced to change your passwords for multiple accounts every six months?
Steve Gibson's 'Security Now' Podcast is an excellent way to keep up with the latest security issues.
Review the section on security and answer the following questions.

(50) 10. Resarch the term 'asymmetric threat'.

(50) 11. Make a brief list of some steps a business can take to defend against 'asymmetric digital security threats'.
