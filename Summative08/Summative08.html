Exercise 2-Project Management

Read Lab 08 'Project Management'
Scan the Wikipedia article on ERP.
Scan the State of Texas ERP plan.
You will spend the class working with your group to develop a detailed project plan based on this lab, as modified below.
The main purpose of this exercise is to experience the process.
Work with your team to make decisions, and record these decisions in an Excel spreadsheet.
You will not use an 'online Clarizen tool' as described in the lab.
Each student is responsible for contributing to the finished product, and each will upload the project plan as a .pdf file.

Clarifications to the instructions
Your team will have four people, the team works together to assign roles:

  • Project Management Office Lead
  • Technical Lead
  • Process Lead
  • Change Lead

Items to consider:
  • Project runs from 1/1/2019 to 12/31/2019
  • Project budget is $35,000,000.00
  • There are four sub-projects or periods, that overlap, as follows:
    • Jan-Feb: Vision, charter and requirements (not addressed in your plan)
    • March-June: Core technical platform
    • March-September: Business configuration
    • May-December: Global deployment
  • Objective is to implement a large scale Enterprise Resource Planning system for a global manufacturing supply chain.

Download this Excel ERPProjectPlan spreadsheet which outlines the sub-projects and tasks.
As a group, decide on the skills, costs and time costs for the tasks, and begin filling in your spreadsheet.
Skills-chosen from a drop-down list, taken from the SFIA listing of Exercise 1
Cost($)-your estimate of the cost in current dollars to purchase, obtain and install the item/task
Cost(hours)-your estimate of the total time in hours for a person or persons of the chosen skillset to install the item/task
